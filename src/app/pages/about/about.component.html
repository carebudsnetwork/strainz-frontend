Strainz - Yield Bearing Non-Fungible Tokens

Collectible NFTs

All Strainz have unique DNA which translates to a specific appearance.

  • Head
  • Body
  • Face
  • Pot
  • Color
  • Accessories
Harvest Strainz Tokens

Over time, each plant produces STRAINZ depending on its growth rate.

STRAINZ is a BEP-20 Token, which means it can be traded freely on the Binance Smart Chain.

They can also be used to buy Strainz NFT plants in the marketplace and are required for breeding.
Breed & Mint NFTs

Two Strainz NFT plants can breed together by burning a certain amount of STRAINZ.

Each Strain has a breeding cost which you have to pay in order to breed two Strainz plants with each other.

After breeding you get a new freshly minted Strain NFT plant, where its growth rate is dependent of its parents.
Marketplace

In the marketplace you can trade Strainz NFT plants, or any other BEP721 NFT on Binance Smart Chain.

Just head over to your wallet, and begin selling your NFTs!

10% of each trade gets burned, to incentivize STRAINZ growers.
